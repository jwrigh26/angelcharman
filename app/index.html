Arcade Angel Bible Action

Join Angel Charman in his quest of righteousness by collecting all the missing scripture bits in each level. Dodging evil minions, and aiming for celestial perfection!

Review the Scriptures

After completing each board, see how many bits of the scripture you gathered, and read over the level's scriptures one more time!
